What to know about Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical type of pasta that originates from Italy.
It is one of the most popular and widely recognized pasta shapes worldwide.
Traditionally made from durum wheat semolina and water, spaghetti is known for its firm texture and ability to hold sauces well.
