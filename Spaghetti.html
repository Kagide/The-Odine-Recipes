Spaghetti

Description

Classic Italian spaghetti dish with a rich, flavorful tomato sauce and tender spaghetti noodles.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil

Steps to cook Spaghetti

  1. Cook spaghetti noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and let it simmer.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and basil.
  6. Mix cooked spaghetti with the sauce and serve with Parmesan cheese.
