Lasagna

Description

A delicious, layered pasta dish with cheese, meat, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes.
  6. Let it rest before serving.
