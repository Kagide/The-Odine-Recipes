Salad

Description

Fresh and healthy salad with a variety of vegetables and a tangy vinaigrette dressing.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and bell pepper.
  3. Top with feta cheese.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
